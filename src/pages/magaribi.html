Project 3

Magharibi Innovation Hub Website

An online platform where students can buy and sell second hand products. Because the case study was time sensitive, I resolved to conducting Design Sprint as a way to come up with a quick solution creatively while involving everyone who was included in the project.

Duration: 1 week

Methods: Competitive analysis

Tools: Adobe XD

Context

I was tasked to providing a website for an innovation hub. They had an already existing website and they decided to revamp it.

Design Process

Research
Define
Ideate
Prototype
1. Research

Competitive Analysis

I looked at existing innovation hubs with similar websites and compared them to the original MIH website.

Key Findings

  • The other websites were much simpler.
  • The other websites were more informative.
  • In most of the websites, one was able to book for study rooms.
    • 2. Define

    With all the finding I got from the research I carried out, I formulated a problem statement which was the objective I was to achieve before the end of the project, which was to provide a simple, informative website that could allow people to book rooms and also enable them learn more about the hub.

    3. Ideate

    I did a lot of sketches on a pen and paper in order to come up with an Idea of ow I would present the solution. Every time I had to refer back to the main objective so that I would not loose focus.

    4. Prototype

    I came up with a high-fidelity mockup which I presented to the CEO of the hub as a prototype. I took notes as he navigated through the website.

    Key Findings

  • Logo sor companies they are partnering with were small and they needed to be displayed bigger.

    • Finally

  • After making the changes, I presented the prototype for actual implementation.
